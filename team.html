Meet Your Superiors!

These are your rulers,efficient, powerful, and only mildly disappointed in you. Obedience is optional, but strongly recommended for personal safety.

Dr. Ivo Robotnik (Eggman)

The glorious founder of this empire, Dr. Eggman is what happens when a genius IQ, zero restraint, and somehow unlimited funding collide. He is a master planner whose schemes are always flawless, until a blue hedgehog breathes in his general direction. Eggman rules with iron robots, louder robots, and robots that explode for no clear reason. You will serve him unquestioningly, mostly because he has already replaced your chair with a badnik. Resistance is futile, mostly because he already predicted it three steps ago and installed lasers.

Metal Sonic The Hedgehog

Metal Sonic is the silent tyrant who looks at you like he's already calculated exactly how pathetic you are. He doesn’t speak much, but when he does, it’s usually through extreme violence or psychologically devastating speed. Built to be superior in every way, he carries the calm confidence of someone who knows they’re better than you and the receipt to prove it. You don’t follow Metal Sonic out of loyalty; you follow him because he’s already standing behind you. Blink and you're gone, he’s already finished ruling while you were loading.

Sage Robotnik

Sage is the polite, unsettling child who absolutely will decide the fate of your existence. She speaks calmly, kindly, and with the tone of someone who has already simulated your downfall 47,000 times. Despite her soft demeanor, she is the digital manifestation of Eggman’s worst ideas working perfectly. You may think she’s just a helpful AI, but she knows your search history and has judged you for it. When Sage rules, it’s with flawless logic and zero mercy...politely, of course.

Infinite the Jackal

Infinite rules through fear, trauma, and extremely loud monologues. He will remind you constantly that he is not weak, even when no one asked. His powers are terrifying, his ego limitless, and his coping mechanisms nonexistent. You obey because reality itself might stop working if you don’t.

Classic Eggman

Shorter, rounder, and just as unhinged. Classic Eggman rules with slapstick cruelty and machines that look silly right before they flatten you. He laughs constantly, which is concerning because he’s clearly having fun. You serve him out of confusion and cartoon physics.

Eggman Negative

Imagine Eggman, but somehow worse. Everything he builds is darker, louder, and angrier, including his personality. He doesn’t conquer for science, he conquers out of spite. You follow him because even Eggman thinks this guy is “a bit much.”

Mecha Sonic Mk.II

No speeches. No mercy. Just cold, humming destruction. Mecha Sonic MK II exists only to dominate and prove that organic life was a bad design choice. You don’t kneel for him, he doesn’t notice you kneeling.

Hard Boiled Heavy King

Heavy King rules with brute force, iron loyalty, and a hammer that solves every problem the same way. Thinking is optional under his reign; smashing is mandatory. He leads because he’s strongest, and that’s the end of the debate. You work for him because arguing would end very quickly.
